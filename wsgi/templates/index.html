What is space-data.

Agencies' open data from orbital and beyond missions, webpages and articles, scientific papers, any other information connected to space exploration and related engineering and astrophysic knowledge: missions, sensors data and engineering, physics, experiments, vectors and probes.

Pramantha platform originates from Project Chronos and integrates information about space missions from NASA, ESA, and JAXA with our learn-by-playing paradigm to let you dive into space knowledge!

What Pramantha can do.

Pramantha is built on Linked Data to let fans access a set of games, data tools and social features. This experience is entirely designed to let people explore space activities in a personal way, with the possibility to share experiments, discoveries and news about events and updated data taken from first-hand space activities.

What are the goals.

The next era of space exploration will be driven by distributed and open exploration ventures. Thousands of rovers and micro-probes will be surfing the System, controlled by many private and public institutions and, eventually, by private citizens, with the goal of collecting and sharing data. Space is too vast to have restricted access: cheap technologies (micro-satellites, citizen science, open research, private launchers), multidisciplinarity, and always more specialized knowledge are taking part to the race. Pramantha is shaping this scenario.
